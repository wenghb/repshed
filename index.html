Frequently Asked Questions

Is TuneCraft really free?

Yes, 100% free. No trials, no premium tiers, no ads. All features are available right now, no account needed.

Does TuneCraft upload my music files?

No. All audio processing happens entirely in your browser using the Web Audio API. Your files never leave your device. There are no servers involved.

What audio formats are supported?

MP3, WAV, OGG, M4A, FLAC, AAC, and WMA — up to 100MB. Basically anything your browser can decode.

Can I slow down music without changing the pitch?

Yes! Speed ranges from 0.1× to 2× with pitch preserved. Hear every note at a comfortable tempo, then work your way up.

Does it work on mobile and tablet?

Yes. TuneCraft is fully responsive and supports touch on iPad, iPhone, and Android devices. Keyboard shortcuts are available on desktop.

What is the Speed Trainer?

It's like interval training for musicians. Set a start speed (e.g. 0.5×), a target (e.g. 1.0×), and a step (e.g. 0.05×). TuneCraft auto-increases the tempo after a set number of loops, building your speed gradually without overwhelming you.

How precise are the loop boundaries?

You can adjust loop start and end points with ±0.1 second precision using the fine-tune controls. You can also type exact timestamps.

Do I need to create an account?

No. There's no account system. Just open the page and start practicing. Your audio never touches a server.
